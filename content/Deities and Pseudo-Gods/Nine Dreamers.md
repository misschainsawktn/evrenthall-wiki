# The Dreamers of Evrenthall

The **Nine Dreamers** 

## ༺ Dharsovann

> [!caption|left]
> ![[z_attachments/icon/dharsovann_chaydanit.png|200]]
> ***Dharsovann Chaydanit***

**Dharsovann Chaydanit** (meaning “_Dharsovann, He Who Was Chosen by Ydani_”) was the legendary **First Emperor of ==Eshayunad==** and is currently the continent's Dreamer. He was a [[Centaur]] from a small village in the West of the continent, who started to get prophetic visions from [[Ydani the Viridian]] when he turned 16 years old. The Amphytrion promised him a reign of peace and prosperity if he would serve them and spread their word among the people of the neighboring areas; Dharsovann decided to do so and follow them, and began his journey through the lands, which would result in the formation of the first three districts of the Empire.

According to the traditional telling of the legend, after another vision, he would elect his successor and later lock himself into his room alongside his three trusted [[Imperial Praetorians|Praetorians]], giving the order not to open the door until exactly five days later, on the 27th anniversary of the first time Ydani spoke to him, and that only the new Emperor should enter the room at first. When that time passed and the door was opened, he and the Praetorians were found alive, but peacefully abandoned to a deep sleep; before the new Emperor could try to wake them up, a vision from Ydani instructed him on what to do with the sleeping creatures, explaining that Dharsovann had been made into a Dreamer and that the Praetorians would protect him even in a different Realm.

Dharsovann's body, alongside those of his Praetorians, was moved to a specially-constructed crypt, called “The Chalice”, the location of which is completely unknown. Dharsovann is said to rest inside a silver sarcophagus with an engraved glass top and a perfect replica of the bedding he fell asleep in inside it, and the Praetorians kneel in front of it with velvet cushions under their knees, leaning over the glass, still wearing their armors and holding their weapons.
