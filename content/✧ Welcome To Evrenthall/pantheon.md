# Introduction to the Pantheon

The **Pantheon of Evrenthall** was, as everything else in the universe, heavily affected by the [[Great Collision]]. It was also significantly reshaped by the [[Senntharet]], which invested the entirety of Evrenthall as an Universe and all of its [[Realms of Existence|Realms]].

There are two types of deities in the Pantheon of Evrenthall, commonly referred to as ***Hegemon*** and ***Amphytrion***. They differ deeply in their very essence, and their role in the order of the world is fundamental to understand the workings of magic (as they are commonly understood to be the origin of the [[Magic Intro|Fluxes of Magic]]) and many other phenomena.
Other creatures are known to be powerful enough to generate Fluxes without being Deities themselves; said beings are usually called ***Pseudo-Gods*** (although other names for them exist), and are sometimes venerated alongside regular Deities.

## Amphytrion

### Founder Amphytrion
Each [[Nine Continents|continent]] of Evrenthall has an Amphytrion regarded to be its “Founder”, the deity who willed the creation or the evolution of the corresponding continent into what it is in the present. The Founder Amphytrion have their own official cults and traditions associated to them, and they are generally the most venerated omes on the continents’ territories.
- [[Ydani the Viridian]], Founder of [[Eshayunad]]
- [[Mydorahm the Guardian]], Founder of [[Hysthyalm]]
- [[Devrée the Alchemist]], Founder of [[Luerien]]
- [[Azidrefia the Flourishing]], Founder of [[Maaghteab]]
- [[Khüritar the Ancient]], Founder of [[Qor'chkis]]
- [[Vaihde the Pioneer]], Founder of [[Tellurväuhr]]
- [[Huitzyocatl the Perpetual]], Founder of [[Xiranitzcaj]]
- [[Ryonnoe the Silent]], Founder of [[Xiu-Renyou]]
