# Introduction to the Pantheon

The **Pantheon of Evrenthall** was, as everything else in the universe, heavily affected by the [[Great Collision]]. It was also significantly reshaped by the [[Senntharet]], which invested the entirety of Evrenthall as an Universe and all [[Realms of Existence|Realms]].

There are two types of deities in the Pantheon of Evrenthall, commonly referred to as ***Hegemon*** and ***Amphytrion***. They differ deeply in their very essence, and their role in the order of the world is fundamental to understand the workings of magic (as they are commonly understood to be the origin of the [[Magic Intro|Fluxes of Magic]]) and many other phenomena.
Other creatures are known to be powerful enough to generate Fluxes without being Deities themselves; said beings are usually called ***Pseudo-Gods*** (although other names for them exist), and are sometimes venerated alongside regular Deities.
