Hello everyone, and welcome to this silly little corner of my tiny allocated room in the World Wide Web! My name is **Jay** (although my IRL friends know me as Ely, it's still me!) and I'm a little bunny on the Internet, figuring out how to get her space in a world sometimes much too big and confusing.

In real life, I am a fifth-year high school student approaching her final exam and nearing her entrance into the world of university. *Fun fact: by being on this little website, you are looking at my "masterpiece", a project we could choose to represent our progress during our time at school. I chose to talk about my experiences with role-playing games (mostly D&D), and Evrenthall as a world is featured too!*

Apart from studying and stressing out, I enjoy drawing, writing, worldbuilding and TTRPGs, as well as many other things that aren't quite fit to list here.

This site here is merely a little part of my wider [Digital Garden](https://placehold.co) (which is still getting built and not public yet), hosted on a different page because of my own little obsessions when it comes to organizing stuff—I often find myself needing to neatly separate some wider areas from the rest in order to keep everything nice and accessible, both to me and to the people who might take a look at my works. 
Feel free to visit that too, if you have the time: I talk about myself, about things I like and about things I'm studying there.

If you wish to communicate with me directly, feel free to reach out, I promise I do not bite! If anything, please excuse me if I don't reply immediately or if I come off as a bit awkward—I am often pretty low on energy and neurodivergent, things that impact my social skills significantly (but I am trying to get better at them!)

## My Friends

While this would *technically* be a solo project, it's already pretty clear from the fact that I plan to DM D&D in this world that it isn't ***really*** a solo project. I couldn't do it without the participation of my lovely friends, who might occasionally pop up here as guest writers and whatnot! I genuinely couldn't do this without them—they're a pivotal part of how Evrenthall is growing, and I love them all with all my heart for their constant help and support and for their creativity :)
