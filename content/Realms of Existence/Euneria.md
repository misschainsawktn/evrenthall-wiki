Falling asleep, one might suddenly find themselves in a place of soft, dancing iridescent lights, verdant and blooming pastures, misty clearings in the woods and crystalline streams of pure water. Such is the atmosphere enveloping all of **Euneria, the Realm of Dreams**, and surprisingly enough, for how unstable Evrenthall still is, its appearance matches its nature: it truly is a place of warmth and fuzzy memories, it truly is a place of light and comfort, it truly is safe and welcoming to all that step within it. 

Euneria lives thanks to the [[Nine Dreamers]], whose sleep keeps it in the form it is supposed to have.

# Peculiar Elements
- there is no sun in Euneria; a permanent magical phenomenon reminiscent of aurora borealis creates the dim, dusk-like light that shrouds the whole Realm 
	- similarly, there is no such thing as seasons or a day-night cycle: it is a permanent sunset in a permanent spring.
- death is impossible while inside it: a person will simply wake up if something happens to their consciousness while it's in the Realm, and they cannot die while sleeping unless they cross one of the openings to [[The Veil]] that are present in every realm
	- more often that not, if a person is meant to pass on, they *will* cross the opening. it sort of pulls them in.

# Notable Locations

## Crystal Citadel
Where the consciousnesses of the Dreamers are said to reside. Made entirely of opalescent stones, shining with the colors of the rainbow.
