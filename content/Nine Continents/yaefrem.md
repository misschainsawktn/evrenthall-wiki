# Geopolitics

Nine islands form the archipelago of Yaefrem, with each one of them being a sovereign state of its own. Still, the Islands are unified by their common belonging to the Yaefremite Commonwealth

## Király 
capital is [[Zedvienyov]]

## Jázdec

## Peshak

## Uzrăugs
